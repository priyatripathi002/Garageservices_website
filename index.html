Work

Vehicle Inspections

Garages often conduct thorough vehicle inspections to assess the overall condition, safety, and functionality of a vehicle. This can include checking for mechanical issues, examining the brakes, tires, lights, and other essential components to ensure the vehicle is roadworthy.

Brake Repairs and Replacements

Garages frequently handle brake repairs and replacements, which are crucial for vehicle safety. This work includes assessing the condition of brake pads, rotors, and other components and making necessary repairs or replacements to maintain the effectiveness of the braking system.

Oil Changes and Fluid Services

Routine oil changes and fluid services are essential maintenance tasks performed by garages. This work involves draining and replacing engine oil, as well as checking and topping up other fluids like coolant, transmission fluid, and brake fluid to ensure the vehicle's engine operates smoothly.
